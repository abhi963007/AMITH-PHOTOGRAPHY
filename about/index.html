Welcome to the visual world through my lens!

I'm Amithkumar Kizhakoloor, a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing architectural beauty, wedding moments, Kerala landscapes and tourism destinations. Photography, for me, is not just a profession; it's a way of life that allows me to express my creativity and connect with the world in a profound way. I'm a proud Limca Book of Records holder for creating world's first photography history calendar.
Passionate, Obsessed with getting the shot "right"
Photography is more than just taking pictures; it's about storytelling. I believe in the power of a single image to convey emotions, tell a story, and evoke memories that last a lifetime. My journey as a photographer has been a continuous exploration of beauty in architectural forms, vibrant wedding celebrations, Kerala's serene landscapes and tourism destinations.
Storyteller, Mentor
Photography, to me, is a form of art that transcends words. It's a medium that allows me to share my perspective, celebrate diversity, and document the fleeting beauty that surrounds us. Whether it's an architectural masterpiece, a joyous wedding celebration, Kerala's natural beauty, or tourism hotspots, I am dedicated to creating images that resonate and leave a lasting impression.
Notable Achievement
I'm honored to be recognized by the Limca Book of Records for creating the world's first photography history calendar. This achievement reflects my dedication to the art and history of photography. I bring this same level of commitment and innovation to every project, ensuring that each client receives photographs that not only capture moments but create historical records of their special occasions.
