EVERY PICTURE HAS A STORY TO TELL
Passionate, Obsessed with getting the shot "right", Storyteller, Mentor. Limca Book of record holder for creating world's first photography history calendar. I specialize in architectural, wedding, and Kerala tourism photography. With an obsessive eye for detail, I transform ordinary scenes into extraordinary visual stories. My work reflects the vibrant culture and breathtaking landscapes of Kerala while bringing a unique perspective to architectural structures.
Photography is more than just taking pictures; it's about storytelling. I believe in the power of a single image to convey emotions, tell a story, and evoke memories that last a lifetime. My journey as a photographer has been a continuous exploration of beauty in architectural forms, vibrant wedding celebrations, and Kerala's serene landscapes.
As a Limca Book of Records holder for creating the world's first photography history calendar, I bring a unique perspective to every shoot. My process involves deep research, meticulous planning, and patient execution to capture that perfect moment. I approach each project with enthusiasm, whether it's documenting the intricate details of Kerala's traditional architecture, capturing the emotional depth of wedding celebrations, or showcasing the natural beauty of God's Own Country.
